Data Protection & Privacy

Your Data, Your Control

We believe in giving users complete control over their personal data. Our platform is built with privacy-first principles:

  • Minimal Data Collection: We only collect data necessary for verification
  • User Consent: Explicit consent required for all data sharing
  • Data Retention: Automatic deletion of data after specified periods
  • Transparency: Clear visibility into how data is used
